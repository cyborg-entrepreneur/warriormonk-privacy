# Privacy Policy for Warrior-Monk

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Overview

Warrior-Monk is a personal health and wellness tracking app. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled.

## Data Collection

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your iPhone** in an encrypted database:

- Daily practice completions and De (virtue) points
- Meal photos and nutrition data
- Weight entries
- Journal entries
- Glucose readings (from screenshots)
- Sleep data (from Oura screenshots)
- Training session logs
- Conversation history with Shifu AI

### Data Sent to Third-Party Services

**Anthropic (Claude AI):**
- Meal photos are sent to Claude AI for food identification
- Oura and Libre screenshots are sent for data extraction
- Conversations with Shifu are processed by Claude AI
- Anthropic's privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

**USDA FoodData Central:**
- Food names are sent to look up nutrition information
- No personal data is transmitted
- USDA privacy policy: https://www.usda.gov/privacy-policy

### Data NOT Collected

- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No location data
- No contact information
- No data shared with third parties for marketing

## Data Storage

- All personal data is stored locally in a Realm database
- The database is encrypted using a 64-byte key stored in iOS Keychain
- API keys are stored securely in iOS Keychain
- Data never leaves your device except for AI analysis features

## Apple HealthKit

If you grant permission, Warrior-Monk can:
- Read: Glucose, sleep, weight, steps, heart rate variability
- Write: Weight entries

HealthKit data is processed according to Apple's guidelines and never leaves your device or is sent to external servers.

## Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you:
- Delete the app
- Clear app data in Settings
- Reset your iPhone

## Your Rights

You can:
- View all your data within the app
- Delete the app to remove all local data
- Revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings
- Choose not to use AI-powered features

## Security

- Database encryption using iOS Keychain-stored keys
- Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) support
- API keys stored in iOS Keychain, not in app code
- HTTPS for all network communications

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
